## ğŸ¯ What is a Reverse Sales Tax Calculator?

A reverse sales tax calculator helps you find the **original price before tax** when you only know the final price (including tax). Unlike regular calculators that add tax to a base price, this tool works backward to reveal the pre-tax amount.

**Example:**
- You paid **$107.25** (final price with tax)
- Your local tax rate is **7.25%**
- Original price before tax: **$100.00**
- Tax amount paid: **$7.25**
